I am grateful for the kindness of others, particularly when they help others who so desperately need it. It reminds me that there is more light than darkness in the world.

I am grateful for a brisk walk with my wife after work to decompress a bit before making dinner this evening. Sometimes, a walk with each other is the next best thing to a date night.

I am grateful that I’ve just finished off the left over turkey by putting it into a pot pie that is about to go in the oven. It’s an easy dinner and we aren’t wasting food when others struggle to put some on their tables.
