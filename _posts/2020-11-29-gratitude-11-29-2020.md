I am grateful that no one in my family has gotten sick during this pandemic. It seems that masks and quarantine really do work. I hope we can keep it this way.

I am grateful to have a job I can do remotely in this pandemic because I haven’t had to worry about paying the mortgage and I can shelter in place. I know many others are not so fortunate.

I am grateful for patience during this pandemic. It doesn’t come easy, but I know that losing my patience with the current situation won’t make it change.
