I am grateful for the album as an art form. I listened to several of my favorite U2 albums start to finish last night and it brought me great joy.

I am grateful for the podcast <em><a href="https://pca.st/podcast/a7af9490-ebe7-0138-e7f9-0acc26574db2" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Transmissions: The Definitive Story</a></em>, which is about the bands Joy Division and New Order. It’s a great trip down memory lane.

I am grateful for music streaming services. I live having access to all my favorite music in the palm of my hand, though I miss liner notes and browsing through the racks at the record store.
