The past few weeks have been somewhat trying -- not so much in terms of not drinking but in terms of feeling overwhelmed by life. It's just been plain busy and hectic.&nbsp;

This has led to some re-evaluation on my part. I haven't figured it all out by any means but I am coming to question many things about the 12 step program.&nbsp;

Lots of people seem to suggest that the key is acceptance and surrender -- that the key is to stop struggling. I get that but &nbsp;it is counterintuitive.&nbsp;

Life is struggle.&nbsp;

Life is suffering.&nbsp;

These are truths.&nbsp;

If you give up the struggle, aren't you giving up life?
