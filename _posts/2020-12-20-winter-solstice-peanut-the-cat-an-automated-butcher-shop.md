We are almost to the <a href="https://www.nytimes.com/interactive/2020/12/20/us/how-to-survive-winter.html?action=click&amp;module=Top%20Stories&amp;pgtype=Homepage">winter solstice</a>, which is cause for celebration. I am grateful that there will gradually be more daylight everyday starting in two days.

It’s grey today, but not too cold. I got out for a walk with my son and we spoke with the woman who’s cat my son has loved forever. She told us that Peanut died a few weeks ago but that he lived to be twenty years old! I’m grateful for the memories of my son sitting in the middle of the street petting Peanut.

I finally cooked the bacon I bought from a <a href="https://applestonemeat.com">24 hour automated butcher shop in Stone Ridge, NY</a> over Thanksgiving. It was delicious and I am grateful to have gotten a single piece while my family devoured the rest.
